Design of an Active transfemoral Prosthesis

I co-founded a team at IIT, focusing on building assistive health solutions at a low cost for the Indian demographic. This project showcases our first prototype of an active polycentric knee, with controller and state-estimation work.

Modelling and System Identification

  1. Solved Non-linear Dynamics using Newton-Euler equations
  2. Solved for Power profiles; performed energy estimation; sized system powertrain
  3. Optimised Patella coordinates for mechanical advantage

State Estimation and Finite State Machine

  1. Used 4 spatially distributed force sensors and 2 IMUs to track user gait
  2. Signal conditioning, digital filtering and offset learning
  3. Developed Supervisory State Machine to switch modes (walking, stair ascent/descent)
